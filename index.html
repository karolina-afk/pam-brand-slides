Introduction

CEO Pamela Doherty

Leadership Team

PCL carries the poise of a seasoned leader and the soul of a storyteller. We listen to what people truly value, observe what they tell us without judgement, and equip people to connect with each other through credible evidence.

Pamela's conviction, built over decades of leadership experience, is that understanding what people truly value is one of the most powerful tools we have for bringing people together.

Grounded & Human

We are living in a world that increasingly exploits our differences. Division is loud. Connection takes work. People Centric Leadership exists because of a conviction.

The brand doesn't shout, oversell, or perform. It shows up fully, speaks with purpose, and always leaves room for others. Like wind, it adapts and flows through resistance with calm, grounded articulation.

Precise & Warm

PCL delivers freely accessible, evidence-first research reports and leadership insights that bridge the gap between abstract academic research and commercial consultancy.

Its unique lens explores not just what people value, but how similar or different they believe others' values to be. Every product is designed to make rigorous evidence feel accessible.

Two Column Layout (Dark)

Quietly Courageous

PCL is independent and self-funded, partnering only with organisations whose values align with its own. It operates across sectors and generations.

Proactively seeking new audiences rather than waiting for them to arrive, it serves as a megaphone for people's voices, never the judge of their values.

Purposefully Warm

Our visual language carries symbols woven covertly throughout the brand identity. Three stars denoting excellence, achievement, and noble aspirations.

Open circles and gathering—people seated in circles, doorways, and thresholds—reinforcing the commitment to never being a closed club.

See the Whole Person

Candid and raw, with warmth and intention. PCL photography should feel like a documentary filmmaker captured a genuine moment of connection.

Subjects are real people in real settings—never actors in staged boardrooms. Lighting is natural and directional; golden-hour warmth that adds depth and honesty.
